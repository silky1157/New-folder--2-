About RICA

REDS INDUSTRY

CHOICE AWARDS

The REDS Industry Choice Awards or RICA is the first ever choice awards exclusively designed for the real estate industry of Saudi Arabia. The RICA is an honest attempt at celebrating the success of industry’s leading establishments that have redefined possibilities and have made a name, synonymous to success. So who better to judge, than the industry itself. Thus, RICA provides a transparent framework that allows stakeholders of real estate industry to choose the best among them, by the stakeholders themselves. So, we invite you to nominate, vote and celebrate this-one-of-a-kind award, for you, by you, to you.
